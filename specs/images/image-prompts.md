# AI Image & Video Prompt Guide

## Purpose
This file contains curated prompts for generating visuals that match Full Solution's premium beauty, fashion, and skincare brand aesthetic. Use these prompts with AI image generation or video tools to create brand assets for the website.

## Brand Visual Style
- Dark luxury with soft glow lighting
- Editorial beauty photography
- Minimal, modern composition
- Rich textures and polished user interface cues
- High-end product showcase with skin and fashion focus

## Core Image Prompts
### Hero Banner
- "Cinematic luxury beauty hero image for a modern skincare fashion brand, dark editorial palette, glowing magazine-style lighting, elegant model with flawless skin, premium beauty product packaging in soft focus, high resolution"

### Product Showcase
- "High-end product display for beauty and skincare, matte black and gold packaging, soft studio lighting, minimalist premium layout, polished and sensual aesthetic, editorial photography style"

### Team / Communication Section
- "Modern creative studio team collaborating on digital beauty products, dark luxury office environment, confident diverse professionals, sleek interface overlays, subtle glow accents, cinematic mood"

### Dark Mode UI Visuals
- "Futuristic dark mode web dashboard for beauty brand, glass-like panels, neon accent highlights, soft gradients, responsive mobile layout, premium UX aesthetic"

### Notifications / Interaction Art
- "Soft motion blur notification card with elegant beauty brand styling, gold accents, dark glass surface, modern UI micro-interaction visual"

## Suggested Video Prompt Directions
### Brand Story Clip
- "Short cinematic brand story video for a luxury beauty business platform, slow cinematic camera pan, dark ambient lighting, product glow, magazine editorial movement, refined minimalism"

### Product Motion
- "Animated product reveal for skincare and fashion line, dark luxury background, floating bottles and jars, soft laser highlights, premium motion graphics"

### Customer Engagement
- "Video showing a business user interacting with a beauty platform, sleek dark UI overlays, notifications appearing, elegant motion transitions, professional brand experience"

## Prompt Usage Notes
- Add style keywords such as `cinematic`, `editorial`, `luxury`, `dark mode`, `soft glow`, `high resolution`, and `minimalist` for consistent brand visuals.
- Use terms like `B2B beauty platform`, `skincare technology`, `fashion editorial`, and `premium product showcase` to match the business orientation.
- When generating interface imagery, include `glassmorphism`, `micro-interactions`, `card layout`, and `responsive design`.
- For video generation models, keep prompts concise but descriptive with motion direction and brand emotion.

## File Structure
- `specs/images/` is reserved for prompt documentation and later asset references.
- Generated images should be stored in a separate `assets/` or `public/` folder once created, not in the spec folder.
