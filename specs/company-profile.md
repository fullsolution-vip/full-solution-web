# Full Solution Company Profile

## Company Overview
Full Solution is a modern B2B beauty, fashion, and skincare company that provides premium products and solutions for businesses in the beauty industry. Originally founded as AEON, the company has been rebranded to Full Solution to better reflect its comprehensive approach to beauty and fashion solutions.

## Mission & Vision
Full Solution aims to be a leading provider of high-quality beauty and fashion products, serving business clients with innovative solutions that enhance their offerings. The company focuses on B2B relationships, providing wholesale products, consulting, and partnership opportunities.

## Products & Services
- Premium skincare products
- Fashion and beauty accessories
- Wholesale distribution services
- Business consulting for beauty brands
- Custom product development

## Target Market
- Beauty salons and spas
- Fashion retailers
- Wholesale distributors
- Beauty product manufacturers
- Business partners in the beauty industry

## Brand Values
- Quality and innovation
- Professional excellence
- Customer-focused service

## History
Originally established as AEON, the company has evolved to become Full Solution, expanding its product line and service offerings to meet the growing demands of the beauty and fashion industry.
